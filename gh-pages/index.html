Scoring Algorithm

Weighted formula. Fully explainable. No ML. Investors trust it because they understand it.

IRR Calculator

Simplified annualised ROI on cash invested during construction. Not full XIRR. Clearly labelled as estimate.
flowchart LR A["Inputs"] --> B["Calculate"] B --> C["Output"] A --> A1["Unit Price (AED)"] A --> A2["Exit PSF (AED/sqft)"] A --> A3["Hold Years"] A --> A4["Payment Plan"] B --> B1["exit_value = sqft * exit_psf"] B --> B2["cash_at_risk = down + construction %"] B --> B3["total_invested = price * cash_at_risk"] B --> B4["net_gain = exit_value - price"] B --> B5["IRR = (1 + gain/invested)^(1/years) - 1"] C --> C1["Estimated IRR %"] C --> C2["Total Invested"] C --> C3["Net Gain"]

Key Insight: Payment Plan Matters

Lower down payment + construction % = less cash at risk = higher IRR on the same property. This is why comparing payment plans is the killer feature.

Sensitivity Table

Shows IRR at 8 exit PSF scenarios: -10%, -5%, 0%, +5%, +10%, +15%, +20%, +30% of current PSF.

Fuzzy Matching Logic

Transaction matcher uses word-overlap scoring between DLD building names and project names:

  • Tokenize both names into significant words (skip "the", "tower", etc.)
  • Compare first 4 words
  • Pre-filter by area word overlap
  • +0.3 boost for substring containment
  • Minimum 0.5 confidence to accept
